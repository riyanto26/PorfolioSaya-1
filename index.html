About Me

T-shirt Designer!

As a T-shirt designer, I have an educational background in design and technical proficiency in software such as Adobe Illustrator. My design style is renowned for its uniqueness, drawing inspiration from fashion trends, contemporary art, and popular culture. My creative process involves research, sketching, and prototyping before delving into the production phase. I comprehend the fashion market, prioritize customer satisfaction, and engage in material selection and collaboration with manufacturers. Online marketing and personal branding are my key focus areas, while I continuously adapt to current trends and stay involved in the T-shirt designer community.
